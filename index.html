Day-wise Itinerary

◆◇◆
1Day 1

Arrive Moscow

Arrival in Moscow. Airport transfer and check-in. Evening at leisure.

2Day 2

Moscow City Tour

Red Square, Kremlin (outside), St. Basil’s Cathedral and Moscow Metro tour.

3Day 3

Moscow – Sergiev Posad – Suzdal

Visit Trinity Lavra of St. Sergius. Drive to Suzdal and explore the town.

4Day 4

Suzdal – Vladimir – Bogolyubovo – Yaroslavl

Visit Vladimir, Assumption Cathedral and continue to Yaroslavl.

5Day 5

Yaroslavl – Kostroma – Yaroslavl

Excursion to Kostroma. Return to Yaroslavl for overnight stay.

6Day 6

Yaroslavl – Rostov Veliky – Uglich – Moscow

Visit Rostov Veliky and Uglich. Evening return to Moscow.

7Day 7

Moscow – St. Petersburg (Sapsan Train)

High-speed train to St. Petersburg. Check-in and evening at leisure.

8Day 8

St. Petersburg City Tour

Peter and Paul Fortress, Hermitage Museum and Palace Square.

9Day 9

St. Petersburg – Pushkin – St. Petersburg

Visit Catherine Palace in Pushkin. Return to St. Petersburg.

10Day 10

St. Petersburg at Leisure

Free day to explore or join optional experiences.

11Day 11

Depart St. Petersburg

Airport transfer for your departure.
